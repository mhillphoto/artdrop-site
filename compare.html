// 03 — What you keep

Automation that does not
take over

Print on demand automation gets a bad reputation because most tools run on their servers, behind their accounts, under their terms. ArtDrop is different by design.

Your API keys stay on your machine
ArtDrop is a desktop app. Your Shopify credentials, your Gelato API key, your AI provider key — all stored locally. Nothing passes through our servers. No data leaves your machine unless you send it to Shopify or a provider yourself.
You review AI copy before publishing
Nothing publishes without a review step. You see every title and description the AI generated before it touches your store. Approve it, edit a sentence, or reject it entirely. The workflow keeps you in the loop without keeping you in the weeds.
You choose which providers to use
ArtDrop connects to Gelato, Printful, and Printify. You decide which ones to activate and in which order. You are not locked into a provider relationship through us — you keep your own accounts, your own contracts, your own pricing.

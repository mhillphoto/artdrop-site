// How we calculated this

Redbubble: Standard tier, default markup. Society6: 5-10% depending on product category (10% shown for prints/framed art). TeePublic pays flat $2-4 per item regardless of retail price — not a percentage. Etsy + Printful includes Etsy's 6.5% transaction fee, 3% + $0.25 payment processing, $0.20 listing fee, and Printful's base cost (~40% of retail). Shopify + Printful uses Shopify's Basic plan payment processing (2.9% + $0.30) plus Printful base cost. ArtDrop assumes you use a print provider at cost and keep everything above production + payment processing. No platform takes a revenue cut. Range reflects different product types and providers. Note: Marketplace platforms (Redbubble, Society6, TeePublic) provide built-in customer traffic. ArtDrop is infrastructure — you bring your own audience via your Shopify/Etsy store or ArtDrop Storefront. * ArtDrop is $197 one-time.

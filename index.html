06 / COMPLETE
Fully automated

// Built by a working artist

I built this because I needed it.

Hundreds of photographs sitting on a hard drive. Each one needed a product listing — title, description, SEO tags, alt text, variants, pricing. Doing it by hand meant an hour per product. Multiply that by a hundred pieces and you've got a full-time job that has nothing to do with making art.

ArtDrop exists because that math didn't work. It was built by a photographer who got tired of the busywork and decided to automate every step between finishing an image and having it live in a store. Every feature in this tool came from a real problem encountered during real drops.

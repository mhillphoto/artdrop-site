Core Pipeline
Drop to published in minutes. Drop an image — AI analyzes it, writes the copy, creates products across your providers, publishes to Shopify, and posts to social. Zero manual steps after initial setup.
Three providers, one drop. Gelato, Printful, and Printify all run simultaneously. Use one or all three — ArtDrop publishes to every provider you've connected from the same drop.
One-click Shopify connection. OAuth flow — click, authorize, done. ArtDrop handles store organization, SEO metadata, tags, and navigation menus automatically on every drop.
Social publishing built in. Instagram, Pinterest, Facebook, Threads, and Bluesky. Connect once, post automatically every time you drop.
Voice Trainer. Teach the AI your writing style in plain conversation. React to copy — "too flowery," "shorter," "never say that word" — and it saves your rules. No forms, no prompts, no guesswork.
Intelligence Engine Beta
Market Weather. Real-time trend temperature pulled from 12+ free data sources across 16 countries. See what's heating up before you drop.
Personalized recommendation cards. Scored opportunities based on your art type, products, and target markets. Multi-source signals get a convergence boost — if five independent sources agree, you see it.
Niche discovery. Cross-niche opportunity detection with 40 novelty seeds. Finds intersections you wouldn't search for.
Entertainment-to-aesthetic mapping. Trending shows and films mapped to art styles and product categories. What's on Netflix this week translates into what to drop next.
Provider comparison alerts. Price, shipping, and quality differences across Gelato, Printful, and Printify — surfaced before you commit to a drop.
Drop calendar. Seasonal timing recommendations based on cultural events, holidays, and trend windows across all 16 tracked countries. Southern Hemisphere inversions handled automatically.
